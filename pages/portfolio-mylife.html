About the project

The goal of this project was to create a web based documentary on a subject of choice. I decided to make my documentary about a charity based in Wigan known as MyLife. MyLife is a charity that helps provide support to families and people with disabilities.

My documentary was aimed at a young teenager named Adam, who was suffering with ADHD and during the documentary it shows how MyLife has helped him on his journey.

Our group, VimFox, consisted of 6 members and we set out to create a guide of the coffee shops, restaurants and bars around the Northen Quarter in Manchester.
