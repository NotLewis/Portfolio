About the project

As part of one of my courses at university, Digital Project Management (DPM), we were assembled into groups of 6 to create a high fidelity working prototype of a digital project of our choice. During this project each member had a chance to be project manager to see what it would be like in the work place managing a team.

In our group we ran a series of sprint meetings (throught the use of SCRUM) to make sure that the product we produced was high quality.

Our group consisted of 6 members and we set out to create a guide aimed towards students coming to university for the first time. We focused on Time management; Finances; Mental Health and events that they might be interested in attending.

What I gained from this project

  • People and project management
  • Planning
  • Budgeting
  • Risk management
  • Estimation
  • Keeping to a deadline
