About the project

As part of our University course in the second year, we were given a task to complete as a group, "Manchester City Council are employing a number of digital agencies to create a variety of topic-specific guides to Greater Manchester."

Included in this project, we also had to produce a number of documents alongside the finished Website such as a competitor analysis and user testing just to name a few.

Our group, VimFox, consisted of 6 members and we set out to create a guide of the coffee shops, restaurants and bars around the Northen Quarter in Manchester.

Objectives

  • Create an original 'About Manchester' logo
  • All media must be original
  • Minimum of 10 web pages
  • Must be responsive
  • Accessible and usable to a broad range of users
  • Fast download speeds
  • Conform to current legal requirements for accessibility
